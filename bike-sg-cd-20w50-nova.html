Is a high-performance multigrade motorcycle engine oil

Is a high-performance multigrade motorcycle engine oil developed with advanced level additives to give superior engine wear prevention and extend the life of the engine. The superior motorcycle-specific cleaning additives prevent sludge formation and engine deposits.

It has excellent lubrication which allows for smooth and quick shifting. It also keeps motorbike crankcases clean and provides better engine safety throughout start-up, warm-up, and typical driving conditions. Under high RPM and high-temperature circumstances, it offers lubrication and motor protection.

Performance Features and Benefits:

  • It protects the engine by forming a film-like structure across all of its components.
  • Advanced additives assist to clean and extend the life of the engine.
  • It has a high level of wet clutch friction stability.
  • It protects the motor from high RPMs and heat.
  • To keep the vehicle stable, it has outstanding thermal and oxidative stability capabilities.
Protection of the Environment | Storage Requirements

It is not recommended that spent oil be poured down the drain or dumped in the ground. To dispose of this oil, contact a reputable collection center.

Avoid storing it at extreme temperatures, such as very hot or very cold. Place it in a shaded or enclosed area
