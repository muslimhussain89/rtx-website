Hydraulic 68

Hydraulic 68 oil is a high-viscosity mineral-based hydraulic oil of premium grade.

Hydraulic 68 oil is a high-viscosity mineral-based hydraulic oil of premium grade. It prevents rusting and corrosion, which may harm automobiles, thanks to its excellent anti-wear and anti-oxidation qualities. Its outstanding anti-wear and anti-rust qualities help to extend the life of the vehicle.

It contains anti-foam additives that inhibit the creation of undesirable stable foam and improve the hydraulic system's performance. It is best suited for use in machining headstocks, airline lubricators, and lightly loaded reciprocating compressors.

Performance Features and Benefits:

  • It protects equipment by creating a lubricating coating around it.
  • It provides outstanding anti-oxidation stability even in the presence of heavy metals like copper and bronze, it provides outstanding anti-oxidation stability.
  • Gear oil is composed comprised of a paraffinic basis with a high viscosity index, which prevents it from thinning down too much at high temperatures.
  • Anti-foaming qualities prevent corrosion of equipment.
Protection of the Environment | Storage Requirements

It is not recommended that spent oil be poured down the drain or dumped in the ground. To dispose of this oil, contact a reputable collection center.

Avoid storing it at extreme temperatures, such as very hot or very cold.Place it in a shaded or enclosed area
