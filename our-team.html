Managing Director & Chairman

Saad Khalid Swati

Mr Saad Khalid Swati - MD & Chairman - RTX Lubricants (Pvt) Ltd.He has over 15 years of experience within the Oil Industry. He started his career in Shell Pakistan in 2009, in Retail Fuel Sales and integrated within the industry, switching to Hascol Petroleum Limited in 2014 within the same category.

Excelling year after year from 2014 to 2021, his 7 years journey from TM to GM houses countless achievements including Outstanding Employee department accredits each year and Best Employee of the Year 2017.

Holding Specialisation in International Marketing from London South Bank University, A Marketeer by heart and soul, Mr. Saad Swati has headed assorted functions including Corporate Marketing, Creative, Hi-Street Lubricants, Non-Fuel Retail and Media. Utilising his diverse experience, he has managed to make a mark in the Lubricants Industry within a short span of time, giving the plethora of players stiff competition.

With specialisation in HR Management, Mr. Sauod has has been heading his family business since the past 15 years and has diversified into chemicals, Lubricants and active trading of various commodities.

He brings with him, a sound knowledge base, varied aptitude and contemporary modus operandi to RTX. The blend of his comprehensive experience in Industrial Lubricants and his diverse skills set will surely add to the rich panel of experts heading RTX.

Chief Operating Officer

Bushra Rehman

Ms. Bushra Rehman - COO, RTX Lubricants (Pvt) Ltd. Graduated from IBA, Karachi in the year 2000 and began her career in Business Management in 2001 with BP Pakistan Exploration & Production Inc., where she won numerous accolades for her outstanding work.

Furthering her professional advancement, she joined Hascol Petroleum Limited in 2015, where her commitment and devotion to the organisation were recognised with yearly promotions, resulting in her progression from Marketing Implementer to Manager Marketing in a short span of 5 years.

Ms. Bushra has over 20 years of experience in the oil industry, from Upstream to Downstream, and has successfully managed a wide range of domains ranging from Marketing, Media Management, Event Management, Communications, Creative and Non-Fuel Retail. She is leading the RTX team with efficient business plans in accordance with market trends by drawing on her extensive expertise from her solid educational background in Business Management and has elevated the organization's brand recognition in the marketplace.
