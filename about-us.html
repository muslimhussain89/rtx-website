about us

RTX is a brand of automotive and industrial lubricants offering a wide range of German formulated and locally blended oils, lubricants & greases.

Our formulation is our differentiator and the key to ensuring excellence in our products as well as unlocking new business frontiers. Sustainability is at the core of everything that we do, as we believe in binding the good in energy to add quality to everyday lives.

Human Resources are our strength and partners for progress. We believe in developing with our partners in addressing the evolving needs in today’s changing Lubricants landscape.
