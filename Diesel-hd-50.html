diesel

HD-50 is a high-performance base oil lubricant

It's a high-performance base oil lubricant with specified additives for supercharged diesel and petrol engines running at high speeds. It complies with all API classification CD standards and protects diesel engines against bearing corrosion and high-temperature deposits. It may be used in a variety of passenger vehicles and truck diesel engines, both turbocharged and not, thanks to the inclusion of good additive packages (to prevent engine complexities). Due to the perfect combination of dispersant and detergent, it is capable of working in extreme situations or during high-speed driving while preventing engine damage.

Performance Features and Benefits:

  • Under high-temperature operating conditions, it prevents the production of dangerous deposits.
  • It possesses anti-oxidant and anti-corrosion properties.
  • It delivers outstanding wear protection even in the most complicated valve train devices.
  • Low viscosity reduces internal engine friction.
  • The engine is safeguarded by a proven anti-wear additive system.
Protection of the Environment | Storage Requirements

It is not recommended that spent oil be poured down the drain or dumped in the ground. To dispose of this oil, contact a reputable collection center.

Avoid storing it at extreme temperatures, such as very hot or very cold. Place it in a shaded or enclosed area
