It's a broad range of diesel engine oils

It's a broad range of diesel engine oils that deliver outstanding performance and fulfill all API CF-4 specifications. It's engineered to work with both older and modern emission designs. It comprises a high-quality detergent component that, together with superior soot management technology, ensures exceptional engine cleaning.

It's used in diesel and gasoline engine fleets, as well as small diesel engines in maritime operations. Its antioxidant, antirust, and antifoaming characteristics help to safeguard the engine and extend its lifespan. It is a multigrade diesel oil that meets all API CF-4 requirements and is designed to lubricate a wide variety of diesel and gasoline engines.

Performance Features and Benefits:

  • Because of its low oxidation and anti-wear qualities, it stands out from the competitors in terms of varied fleet capabilities.
  • Between prolonged drain service intervals, it provides trouble-free operation.
  • It possesses anti-oxidant and anti-corrosion properties.
  • It delivers outstanding wear protection even in the most complicated valve train devices.
  • Low viscosity reduces internal engine friction.
  • The engine is safeguarded by a proven anti-wear additive system.
Protection of the Environment | Storage Requirements

It is not recommended that spent oil be poured down the drain or dumped in the ground. To dispose of this oil, contact a reputable collection center.

Avoid storing it at extreme temperatures, such as very hot or very cold. Place it in a shaded or enclosed area
