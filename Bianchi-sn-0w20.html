Synthetic motor oil - Tailored to meet the specific requirements of engine manufacturers.

BIANCHI 0W20 is a range of top-tier motor oil that is formulated using breakthrough RTX Synthetic Technology, yielding a motor oil that actively helps keep engines clean. Active cleansing technology has always been at the heart of RTX BIANCHI. With RTX Synthetic Technology, this product offers even higher levels of cleansing and protection.

Performance Features and Benefits:

  • Unsurpassed wear and sludge protection
  • Leaves pistons 50% cleaner than the industry standard
  • Enhanced fuel economy, with a corresponding reduction in emissions
Protection of the Environment | Storage Requirements

It is not recommended that spent oil be poured down the drain or dumped in the ground. To dispose of this oil, contact a reputable collection center.

Avoid storing it at extreme temperatures, such as very hot or very cold.Place it in a shaded or enclosed area
