RTX offers 20W50 CF which is a multi-grade synthetic engine oil

RTX offers 20W50 CF which is a multi-grade synthetic engine oil that provides lubrication to the engine. It excels in controlling high temperatures and providing thermal strength while ensuring that oil thickening is kept to a minimum. It's made using contemporary preservative technologies and offers oxidative strength to keep engine damage to a minimum.

It maintains engine cleanliness, allowing your vehicle to operate at its best. The existence of an anti-wear additive solution extends the engine's life by providing a protective coating on contact surfaces.

Performance Features and Benefits:

  • It prevents corrosion of engine components.
  • Under high-temperature operating conditions, it prevents the production of dangerous deposits.
  • It possesses anti-oxidant and anti-corrosion properties.
  • It generates the maximum quantity of energy.
Protection of the Environment | Storage Requirements

It is not recommended that spent oil be poured down the drain or dumped in the ground. To dispose of this oil, contact a reputable collection center.

Avoid storing it at extreme temperatures, such as very hot or very cold. Place it in a shaded or enclosed area
